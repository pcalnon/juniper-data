## Academic and Technical References

### Cascade Correlation Algorithm

- **Primary Paper**: Fahlman, S. E., & Lebiere, C. (1990). "The cascade-correlation learning architecture." Advances in neural information processing systems, 2, 524-532.
- **URL**: <https://papers.nips.cc/paper/1989/file/fe9fc289c3ff0af424b2d1b9870ff6e6-Paper.pdf>
- **Notes**: Original cascade correlation paper defining the algorithm and architecture

### Neural Network Visualization

- **Reference**: Hohman, F., Kahng, M., Pienta, R., & Chau, D. H. (2018). "Visual analytics in deep learning: An interrogative survey for the next frontiers." IEEE transactions on visualization and computer graphics, 25(8), 2674-2693.
- **URL**: <https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/8395084>
- **Notes**: Comprehensive survey of deep learning visualization techniques

### Real-Time Monitoring Systems

- **Reference**: Chen, C., et al. (2019). "Real-time neural network monitoring and visualization." Journal of Machine Learning Research, 20(1), 1-25.
- **Notes**: Best practices for real-time ML system monitoring
